About Us

Born in December 2015 in Sonipat, Haryana, this NGO stemmed from the aspiration of building a nation of selfless youth with high spirits of social services. We believe that Seva should not be the dharma of only elderlies, rather young minds must be at the forefront of a revolution. When empowered, they can be the key agents for development and peace. While stumbling our way forward, a dedicated and passionate team of volunteers was created who helped the foundation in achieving great heights. Our foundation completely centers around the future of our nation- youth, we constantly look forward to ways in which we can inculcate the sense of social work among them. Giving money to the needy or feeding the hungry are not only ways of Seva, but making a sad face smile is also considered as Seva. We wish to see the youth working for a better tomorrow. We believe that only a life lived for others is a life worthwhile.

There are various NGOs doing the work of Seva in our country but the question still remains that what our youth is doing in this field and also how much guidance do they need. Keeping all of this in mind, we started the YUVA & SEVA FOUNDATION.

Mission Statement

We aim to encourage the youth to help and serve the underprivileged masses of our society. We also look forward to the development of patriotism and awareness towards ecological and environmental welfare.

Leaving the responsibility of national development in the hands of the youth through associating them with social service activities.
